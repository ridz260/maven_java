Projects

•	AI-Powered Wardrobe Designer:
This project utilizes AI to create personalized wardrobe suggestions, tailored outfit recommendations based on user preferences, body 
type, and occasions. It helps users easily design their wardrobe by recommending clothing that suits their style, fit, and current 
trends.

•	Meeting Summarization:
This project leverages natural language processing (NLP) to automatically summarize meetings, extracting key points, action items, and
 decisions made. It helps teams save time by providing concise, actionable summaries from lengthy meetings, ensuring important 
 information is captured and shared efficiently.
